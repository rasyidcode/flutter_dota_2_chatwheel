The default chat wheel composition.

The Chat Wheel is an in-game interface tool for quickly sending messages to teammates. By holding down the assigned hotkey, moving the mouse in any of the eight directions and releasing the hotkey, a predetermined message gets sent to the team chat. Players are able to change the messages in the Chat Wheel to any of the 87 available phrases. The Chat Wheel can also be used to send emoticons and graphical sprays.

Dota Plus subscribers can equip sound effects, emoticons, and hero-specific lines, which are earned by leveling up the corresponding hero.

Usage[edit]

To send a message using the Chat Wheel, hold down the hotkey and move your mouse in any of the eight given directions. When you release the Hotkey, the message will be sent to your teammates. Messages sent from the Chat Wheel have a ▶ distinctive arrow in front of them and appear in players' local language setting, making it an invaluable tool for communicating with players who do not speak your language. Muted players can still use the Chat Wheel.

To change the phrases or the Chat Wheel hotkey, navigate to the CONTROLS tab of the OPTIONS screen and click CHAT. It is also possible to bind individual Hotkeys to the default Chat Wheel phrases there.

Phrases[edit]

Sample output from the chat wheel.

There are currently 87 available phrases. This table lists them, as well as their Console equivalent.

By using the bind <hotkey> "<console_command_in_quotes>" command in console, it is also possible to create Hotkeys for non-default phrases, e.g. bind k "chatwheel_say 1" to make K output ▶ Careful! in chat.

Unlisted[edit]

Quick chat lines for missing enemies.
Quick chat line for item purchases.
Quick chat lines for ability status.

The following phrases can only be triggered through other means than the Chat Wheel. The game will automatically fill in which item, hero, or ability based on what was clicked. They do not have console commands associated with them.

Phrase Activating
Missing Status
Hero is missing! Alt+ Left Click an enemy hero's portrait at the top of the screen.
Hero has returned! Ctrl+Alt+ Left Click an enemy hero's portrait at the top of the screen.
Enemy Hero will respawn in x seconds! Alt+ Left Click an enemy hero's portrait at the top of the screen, that is currently dead.
I will respawn in x seconds! Alt+ Left Click your respawn timer, either where the health bar is, or at the top of the screen.
Abilities and Items
I will purchase ▶ Item Alt+ Left Click an item in the shop.
Need x gold for Item Alt+ Left Click an item set in Quick Buy without having enough Gold to purchase it.
I have enough gold for Item Alt+ Left Click an item set in Quick Buy with enough Gold to purchase it.
Ability/Item ▶ Ready ( Level Level ) Alt+ Left Click an ability or item icon.
  • It has to be ready for this message to appear. When clicking on an ability icon, the level will be shown.
Ability/Item ▶ On Cooldown ( x seconds remain ) Alt+ Left Click an ability or item icon.
  • It has to be on cooldown for this message to appear.
Ability/Item ▶ Not Enough Mana Alt+ Left Click an ability or item icon. You need to have insufficient mana and the ability or item must not be on cooldown for this message to appear.
Ability ▶ Not Learned Alt+ Left Click an ability icon for an ability that you have not learned yet.
Beware Hero ▶ Ability Alt+ Left Click an ability icon of an enemy hero.
Enemy Hero has ▶ Item Alt+ Left Click an item in an enemy hero's inventory. This line may also be triggered on an enemy courier.
Hero Status
(Enemy Hero) affected by: Buff/Debuff Alt+ Left Click on a buff or debuff indicator on your hero, or an enemy hero.
(I have / Enemy Hero has) x% HP y% Mana. Alt+ Left Click on your health or mana bar, or an enemy's.
(I have / Enemy Hero has) x HP y Mana. Ctrl+Alt+ Left Click your health or mana bar, or an enemy's.
(I need / Ally needs) x XP to reach Level y Alt+ Left Click the experience bar of an allied hero.
Enemy Hero is Level x Alt+ Left Click the experience bar of an enemy hero.
Glyph of Fortification
Glyph of Fortification ▶ Ready Alt+ Left Click on the Glyph button.
Glyph of Fortification ▶ Don't Use yet Ctrl+Alt+ Left Click the Glyph button.
Glyph of Fortification ▶ On Cooldown ( x seconds remain ) Alt+ Left Click the Glyph button when Glyph is on cooldown.
Buyback Status
Buyback ▶ Ready Alt+ Left Click your gold counter, or buyback button while dead.
  • Buyback must be ready, and you must have sufficient gold.
Buyback ▶ Not Enough Gold (Need x) Alt+ Left Click your gold counter, or buyback button while dead.
  • You do not have enough gold for buyback.
Buyback ▶ On Cooldown (Time remaining) Alt+ Left Click your gold counter, or buyback button while dead.
  • Buyback must be on cooldown.
